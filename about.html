My Bio

I am currently a Sophmore student at Brigham Young University living in Provo, UT. I am a Pre-Business major and looking to apply to the Accounting program during the summer. I play Lacrosse for BYU and enjoy competition and athletics that come with playing a collegiate sport.

I was born in Los Angeles, California but was raised in Pleasant Grove, Utah as my parents moved there when I was still a baby. I consider myself to be from Los Angeles though I have spent the majority of my life here in Utah. I enjoy following the Los Angeles sports teams and going to sporting events. I enjoy spending time with my family and friends as well. I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints and am deeply involved and devoted to my faith.
